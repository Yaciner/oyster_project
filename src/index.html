Babeleir de bretagne

Introduction to Babeleir

The Brussels Beer Project especially remade this beer for you, lovers of france and sea. The Babeleir De Bretagne is a combination of delicious beer and fresh oysters.

We hope you also love scuba diving, because if you do, you and your crew will be the best candidates for an amazing contest to win an NYE reception!

Can you already taste the bitter sweet in your mouth? I bet. It’s a new recipe and we know you’ll love it, so scroll down and discover more about our new Babeleir edition & our NYE contest.

A little history

History of Babeleir and Scuba Diving

Althought the idea of combining beer & oysters did not come from Brittany, we have an oysterfarm in Brittany and it has the best oysters ever! We made sure to combine exactly with the best to provide excellent quality to you.

Great thing that we have scuba divers all over the world loving this beer, are you one of them? Because if so, gear up to get divin'!
